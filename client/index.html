Free Online Planning Poker for Agile Teams

Real-time Scrum Poker for agile estimation — create a room, share the link, and estimate story points together using Fibonacci cards. Free, open-source, no sign-up, unlimited rooms.

How it works

  1. Create a room — one click, no registration.
  2. Share the link with your Scrum team.
  3. Estimate together — everyone picks a Fibonacci card, reveal at once, discuss, and agree on the story-point estimate.

Why teams use it

Planning Poker turns sprint planning into fast, consensus-based agile estimation. It works for co-located, hybrid, and remote teams, keeps estimates unbiased (cards are revealed simultaneously), and uses the Fibonacci scale (0, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21…) to reflect uncertainty in larger tasks.

Frequently asked questions

What is Planning Poker?
A consensus-based, gamified estimation technique where agile teams estimate the effort of backlog items by privately choosing a card and revealing all at once.
What is the Fibonacci sequence used for?
The Fibonacci-like deck (1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13…) gives growing gaps that reflect rising uncertainty in bigger stories — our cards use it by default.
Is it free? Do I need to sign up?
Completely free and open-source — no registration, no paywall, unlimited rooms.
How does it fit into Scrum sprint planning?
Teams size user stories in story points during sprint planning; the estimates feed velocity and sprint scope.
Can remote teams use it?
Yes — estimates are revealed in real time, so distributed teams share one room link and estimate together.
